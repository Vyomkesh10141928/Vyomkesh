About Me

Vyomkesh Buch is a birder from surat who thinks that any place in the world is a great spot to watch birds.He got intrested in birding during lockdown(May 2020) by trying to take photograph of birds with mobile.He got introduced to Ebird by exploring different websites to learn more about birds.He admires all species but his favorite birds include woodpeckers,warblers,owls etc.He wants to see 1000 species from India.He likes to bird with others but also birds alone sometimes.He is fortunate to have few trees near his home which provide nesting for many species of birds.He has observed and made a theory on breeding of Jungle Babblers(Turdoeis Striata).Along with birding,he also records bird songs and does some nature photography.He also makes websites and sometimes apps(not yet released).
